Discover why direct mail retargeting works so well.

Today's retargeting strategies rely primarily on a mix of email and display ads to re-engage those who have shown interest by visiting your website.

As a result, more companies are turning to channels such as direct mail to drive this incremental growth.

In this eBook we:

  • Address the challenges impacting the success of today's retargeting strategies
  • Share insights as to how direct mail overcomes these challenges
  • Introduce direct mail retargeting - what it is, how it works, and how to integrate it into your current retargeting strategy
